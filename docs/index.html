Features

Control the displays macOS ignores

Brightness, Any Display

Dim or brighten external and non-Apple monitors that macOS normally locks. Hardware DDC control when the panel supports it, seamless software dimming when it does not.

Color Temperature

Warm the screen for night, cool it for day. Smooth color temperature on every connected display, set independently per monitor.

Resolution & HiDPI

Unlock crisp HiDPI scaling and resolutions your display can do but macOS hides, including retina-style modes on non-Apple panels.

Per-App Profiles

Bind a brightness level or a full preset to an app. Corvus Display applies it automatically the moment that app comes forward.

Presets

One click switches brightness, warmth, and color profile together. Ready-made Reading, Movie, Gaming, and Evening presets, plus your own.

Keep Awake

Stop the display from sleeping during long reads or presentations. Optionally hold it awake only while a chosen app is running.

Sleep Timer

Send the screen, or the whole Mac, to sleep after a countdown. Lock, restart, and shut down options are built in.

Menu Bar & Hotkeys

Everything lives in the menu bar. Global keyboard shortcuts and media-key brightness control put every adjustment a keystroke away.

Multi-Display

Per-monitor control, mirroring with an automatic safety net, and a visual arrangement view for multi-display setups.

Ambient adaptation

Optionally adapt each display's brightness to its own on-screen content, so dark and bright scenes stay comfortable.

Night Shift & True Tone control

Toggle and tune the system color features alongside Corvus Display's own warmth, from one place.

Private by design

No account, no telemetry, no tracking. Corvus Display runs entirely on your Mac and talks to nothing.
