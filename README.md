The display controls macOS keeps for its own screens, brought to every monitor. Brightness, color temperature, color profiles, and resolution for external and non-Apple displays, all from the menu bar. Native **SwiftUI**, hardware **DDC/CI** where the panel supports it, smooth software dimming everywhere else, and zero tracking. One-time purchase with a 14-day free trial.

## Features

- **Brightness on any display.** Dim and brighten external and non-Apple monitors macOS normally locks, over hardware DDC when supported and seamless software dimming when not.
- **Go darker than the minimum.** A gamma and overlay dimming layer takes the screen well below the panel's hardware floor for comfortable late-night brightness.
- **Color temperature.** Smooth, per-display warmth for day and night, with Warm, Paper, and Dark-room tint styles.
- **Color profiles.** Assign any installed ICC profile per display and switch between them in a click.
- **Resolution and HiDPI.** Unlock crisp HiDPI scaling and resolutions a display can do but macOS hides, including retina-style modes on non-Apple panels.
- **Per-app profiles.** Bind a brightness level or a full preset to an app, applied automatically when it comes forward.
- **Presets.** Switch brightness, warmth, and color profile together. Ready-made Reading, Movie, Gaming, and Evening presets, plus your own.
- **Schedules and clock mode.** Ramp brightness and warmth across the day from your sunrise and sunset.
- **Auto-brightness.** Follow the ambient light sensor, or adapt each display to its own on-screen content.
- **Keep awake.** Prevent display sleep during long reads or presentations, optionally only while a chosen app runs.
- **Sleep timer.** Send the screen, or the whole Mac, to sleep after a countdown, with lock, restart, and shut down options.
- **Multi-display and mirroring.** Independent control per monitor, mirroring with an automatic safety net, and a visual arrangement view.
- **Night Shift, True Tone, and Dark Mode.** Toggle and tune the system color features alongside Corvus Display's own warmth.
- **Fix washed-out monitors.** Force full RGB range and 8-bit color to correct the dull, grey look some displays show over HDMI and USB-C.
- **Hardware DDC controls.** Drive the monitor's own contrast, volume, and input source over DDC.
- **Black out and FaceLight.** Blank an unused display, or flood the screen with warm light for video calls.
- **Menu bar, hotkeys, and media keys.** Global shortcuts, scroll-to-adjust on the menu-bar icon, and brightness-key control for external displays.
- **Shortcuts and automation.** App Intents and a local control API for Shortcuts, scripts, and Stream Deck-style tools.
- **33 languages.** Fully localized with a built-in language picker, including right-to-left support for Arabic and Hebrew.
- **Built for privacy.** No account, no telemetry, no tracking. Runs entirely on your Mac.

## What makes Corvus Display special

🖥️ **Made for the displays Apple forgot.** macOS gives third-party monitors almost no control. Corvus Display brings Apple-grade brightness, color, and resolution adjustment to external and non-Apple panels.

🌗 **Brightness below the hardware floor.** Most monitors will not dim far enough for a dark room. A gamma and overlay layer takes them lower, smoothly, without a grey backlight wash.

🎨 **Real color control.** Per-display color temperature, ICC profiles, eye-comfort tints, plus Night Shift and True Tone in one place.

🧩 **Automation that fits your day.** Per-app profiles, presets, sunrise-to-sunset schedules, ambient and content-adaptive brightness, Shortcuts, and a local control API.

🖱️ **Lives in the menu bar.** Every control a click, scroll, or hotkey away, with multi-display support and mirroring safety nets.

🛡️ **Built for privacy.** Zero accounts, zero telemetry, zero analytics. License validation happens locally against a Keychain-stored key. No phone-home.
